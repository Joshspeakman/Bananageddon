Monkey Sprite Options

This page is just for picking a monkey sprite direction. Each card shows the same monkey in idle, throw, and arms-up poses. Pick the option number you like best and I’ll wire that style into the game. Options 21-40 push much harder toward that early arcade barrel-platform gorilla silhouette. Options 41-48 are a completely redrawn sprite built around the reference image — dark slate-blue fur, rounded dome head merging into the shoulders, cream beard at the chin, and contrasting pink palms.
Classic baseline is option 01. Options 21-40 explore hunched shoulders, longer arms, squatter legs, wider jaws, and chunkier arcade posture. Options 41-48 rebuild the sprite from scratch to match the reference image look.
